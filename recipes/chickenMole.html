My Chicken Mole Recipe

A delicious Chicken Mole dish to crave for

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs of chicken thighs (Boiled/Baked)
  • 8 oz jar of Doña Maria mole sauce base
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 3.1 oz tablet of Mexican hot chocolate
  • 2 Tbsp peanut butter

Cooking Steps

  1. Put your chicken in a pot with water and start boiling till it's complete.
  2. After the chicken is complete, go ahead and put 4 cups of the chicken broth left over into a measuring cup.
  3. With a new pot, pour the chicken broth, Doña Maria mole sauce, peanut butter and chocolate into the pot. Let all the ingredient meld together and make sure the chocolate is melted! Once everything looks great, bring the sauce to a simmer and get ready for the next part.
  4. Add the boiled chicken into the mole pot and let that simmer for 15 minutes or until you feel that both chicken and mole are nice and blended.
  5. Now it's time to grab a plate! This dish pairs great with mexican rice and beans. Go ahead and dig into the pot for some mole chicken.
  6. And you are all done, enjoy your chicken mole!
